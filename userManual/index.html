We decided to make a platforming game where players will get to traverse across space and travel from planet to planet. Users will play as a wandering astronaut and begin their journey at a starter planet where they will be given a chance to learn how to play this game before progressing to more and more difficult planets as their adventure continues. Each planet will have differing themes, ranging from a desolate ice planet to a planet set in a cave. The player will learn to face each of these unique challenges throughout their journey to find what is at the end of the solar system.
